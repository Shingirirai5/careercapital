About Us

Whether you realize it or not, your career informs your lifework. It has the power to open and close the doors that will determine your income, your lifestyle, your health, your availability for non-professional pursuits, the quality of your relationships and ultimately your legacy.
In short, the impact of your career is not limited to what happens between 9am and 5pm.
Instead, it ripples across your whole life, affecting everything from what time you wake up in the morning to when you and where you fall asleep at night

Our Vision
  • To elevate the professional consciousness in every person mobilizing our collective agency for the benefit of society’s greater good.
  • To mobilize collective agency for the benefit of society’s greater good through elevating the professional consciousness of every person.
  • Elevating the professional consciousness of every person to mobilise collective agency for the benefit of society’s greater good.

Aims & Objectives
  • To enable individuals to create more value in their work and to extract more meaning from their vocational experiences.
  • To grow an appreciation for work as an inseparable part of our respective contributions to humanity.
  • To transform the perceptions individuals have about the significance of their day to day decision on their career’s.
  • To democratize access to anecdotal information about personal and professional development.
  • To unlock a desire in every person to make a positive impact through their work.
